Our Mission and Vision

Mission

The mission of the Girls' Computer Programming Club is to engaged women in technology by providing education, support, and networking opportunities, aiming to bridge the gender gap in the tech industry and inspire more women to pursue careers in computer programming.

Vision

The vision of the Girls' Computer Programming Club is to create an inclusive and diverse tech community where women are equally represented and empowered to lead and innovate in the technology industry.
